Fattori

The story of the Fattori wine making estate began in the early 20th century with Antonio Fattori. He had a vision to plant vines on the picturesque hills near the village of Terrossa, nestled between Verona and Vicenza. Fast forward to today, three generations of the Fattori family continue the legacy. They proudly carry on Antonio's dream, serving as experienced wine makers and passionate land owners. The Fattori estate stands as a testament to their dedication and expertise, crafting wines that reflect the rich history and tradition passed down through the generations. With each bottle, you taste the fruits of their labor and the love they have for their craft.

The Fattori vineyards span across a range of altitudes, stretching from 150m to 450m above sea level. This diversity allows them to cultivate different vine varieties suited for various wines. In the volcanic slopes of the Alpone valley, you'll find the "white" vineyards. Here, Garganega, Durella, and Trebbiano grapes thrive, producing wines with unique character and flavors. As you journey further south towards the Po Valley plain, the landscape transitions, and Pinot Grigio and Sauvignon Blanc take center stage. These grape varieties flourish in the region, offering distinct expressions that capture the essence of the terroir. From the rolling hills to the fertile plains, the Fattori vineyards embrace the natural beauty of the land, carefully selecting vine varieties at different elevations to craft wines that showcase the diversity and richness of the region.
