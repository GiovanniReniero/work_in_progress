Poggio delle Grazie

Nestled on a gentle, sloping hill in the southern region of Lake Garda, you'll discover the enchanting Poggio delle Grazie vineyard. It's a place of captivating beauty where, on clear days, the highest point offers a breathtaking view. From there, you can witness the mesmerizing sight of the sun's radiant reflection shimmering upon the distant surface of lake Garda's waters. It's a truly awe-inspiring experience that connects you with nature's wonders.

Poggio delle Grazie, translates to "Small Hill of Graces". The name is inspired by the harmonious blend of nature and spirituality that surrounds the wine producing estate.The winery's logo features a beautiful, stylized interpretation of a window found on the facade of the local village church. Local wine making tradition, mineral rich soils and warm Mediterranean climate are Poggio's heritage that shape their wine making journey and unique produce.

A captivating blush-colored wine with orangish reflections. It delights with a fine, persistent fizz. Enticing aromas of mandarin and peaches. The taste is a savory and enveloping delight, with sweet sensations complemented by a hint of minerals.

Great with Pizza or white meat.

A delightful rosé wine, blush in color with violet reflections. Enticing aromas of small red fruits and invigorating eucalyptus. A beautiful balance between softness and mineral flavors, with hints of black currant and citrus. This refreshing rosé wine embodies the essence of the Corvina and Rondinella blend, creating a truly captivating and invigorating sensation. It's a perfect choice for those seeking a refreshing and delightful rosé experience.

Ideal for pizza and fish based dishes or with a curry.
