Zambon-Vulcano

Zambon-Vulcano is another prized winery placed at the heart of the Durella producing capital of Roncà, in the Alpone Valley. Placed between the towns of Verona and Vicenza, at the heart of the Veneto region of Italy, the Zambon-Vulcano estate can boast some of the finest vineyards of this mineral rich area. Ideally positioned in elevation for a cooler mediterranean climate and surrounded by rich micro-fauna of the Lessini hills, Federico Zambon and his team have worked hard and passionately to produce some of the most exquisite white wines in Italy.

The Zambon-Vulcano team are firm believers in the "UltraBio" approach to grape growing in which the local indigenous micro-flora and micro-fauna are encouraged to thrive. Careful attention is also given to new avant-garde agronomic practices without however, discarding local traditional methods that are refined and adjusted to modern day challenges. To this day the "pergola veronese" features prominently in the Zambon estate.

The Vulcano Line of wines (Volcano project), is a tribute to the Tertiary basic lava flows that feature so prominently in the Alpone Valley and around Roncà. Three volcano domes are an integral part of the landscape around Roncà as a reminder ofg its turbulent geological past. Some excellent Garganega vines deliver outstanding Soave wine and of course there is also top level Durello Sparkling wine enjoy from Zambon as well.

Bright and pale lemon in color, this sparkling wine captivates with its creamy texture and delicate effervescence. The enticing aroma reveals an array of stone fruits, complemented by subtle hints of sulfur and gentle mineral notes that evoke a sense of the earth.

On the palate, it is dry, offering a refreshing and well-balanced experience. The lingering aftertaste delights with a tangy acidity that harmonizes perfectly with its fruity undertones.

it pairs splendidly with seafood, this wine enhances the flavors of the sea and brings a touch of elegance to any dining event.

Each year, the Zambon-Vulcano estate meticulously handpicks grapes from their sun-kissed, south-facing vineyards. These grapes, chosen with utmost care, form the foundation of their exceptional Durello Brut sparkling wine. Using the revered Champenoise Method, this sparkling gem undergoes a patient journey of 36 months on the lees, resulting in a truly extraordinary creation. It's a testament to the harmonious marriage between the unique climate, volcanic soil, and the expert touch of Federico Zambon himself. Prepare to be enchanted by this remarkable sparkling wine, a testament to nature's gifts and human craftsmanship.

Exuberant, sleek, elegant, and an extremely volcanic sparkling wine. A definite must!
