Peri Bigogno

In 1946, Graziella and Adriano Peri purchased a charming country house in Castenedolo, Lombardy. The presence of a historical cellar beneath their new home and the surrounding vineyards greatly inspired Adriano to uphold the local tradition of wine production.

In 2008, their son Andrea Peri embarked on an ambitious internationalization project. After a few years, Peri Wine products successfully reached three continents across the globe. Today, their wines have made their way to the UK, captivating wine enthusiasts as part of the Bandits collection.

Situated near Lake Garda, Castenedolo owes its unique landscape to the advance and retreat of the Adige Valley glacier. Over the course of four different glacial events the glacier gave birth to the lake GArda and also shaped the undulating moraine hills and clay terrace that envelop it. The waters of Lake Garda, coupled with the local gentle pre-alpine breeze, creates a cool Mediterranean microclimate. The local grape varieties flourish under these exceptional conditions, resulting in wines with truly remarkable flavors.

Peri's Sparkling Chardonnay has a bright straw yellow colour with golden refections. The perlage is fine and persitent almost creamy and so very pleasant.

You do get the bread crust aroma surrounded by rich bouquet scents with delecate notes of dry fruit.

Well it’s a Chadonnay so fits well with almost any kind of food. 36 months o the lees, 75% Pinot Noir, 25% Chardonnay.

Peri's Talento Rosè is a fine elegant soft pink rosè, with a refined and persistent perlage.

Notes of nuts, sweet almonds and spices, with hints of wild strawberries blend marvelously together.

Traditional Champenoise method, 36 months on the lees. 25% Chardonnay and 75% Pinot Noir.

We suggest enjoying it on its own or with appetizers or even pizza.

Talento Chardonnay Pas Dosè is yellow with golden reflections and a very fine and abundant perlage. It’s a pas dosè, so no sugars and not for every palate, but we highly recommend it.

It’s almost a wild sparkly, rich in acidity and aromas, with a lovely perfume of bread crust, and yet it follows its own rules, giving notes of hazelnut and dried fruit like peach apricot and yellow apple.

Traditional Champenoise method, 36 months on the lees, 100% Chardonnay

This Chardonnay goes well with rich dishes and fried food, so just pick your fav and enjoy the pairing.
