Cortenera
"Black Courtyard"

Cortenera is a relatively new winery, forward looking and technologically advanced but at the same time committed to traditional vinification methods. The wine making process at Cortenera is centred on environmental sustainability, and the wine is produced exclusively from Durella and Garganega vines.

The winery is located at the heart of the Veneto Region, on the Tertiary volcanic hills of Gambellara, between the noble towns of Verona and Vicenza. Here, Mount San Marco with its black igneous rocks, provides the rich mineral nutrients upon which the Durella and Garganega grapes thrive and awards the sparkling wines they yield with a distinctive edge.

Garganega has delicate scents of almond and white flowers. Durella has a stronger character with an enhanced natural acidity.

Cortenera's Sparkling Brut has an intense yellow colour, almost golden. The foam is thick and creamy and the perlage is delicate but decisive. It yields rich aromas of tropical fruits, pineapple, peach and banana with fine spicy notes.

This wine is perfect in every occasion: from aperitifs to refined meals.

Cortenera's 30 month Sparkling Brut has an intense yellow colourwith a fine perlage and a dry finish taste with notes of tropical fruits.

It complements cheese, fish and white meat perfectly.

Cuvee Ginevra's sparkling wine is is golden-yellow and yields aromas of honey, exotic fruits and chestnuts. In taste it reveals an intriguing mix of spice and notes of toasted bread.

Named after the wine producer's daughter, this wine is perfect with light, fine cuisine dishes.
