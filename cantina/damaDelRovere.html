Dama del Rovere

Welcome to the enchanting world of Dama del Rovere winery, where passion, tradition, and excellence intertwine to create extraordinary sparkling wines. Nestled at heart of the Veneto region between the historical towns of Verona and Vicenza, the scenic countryside of the Alpone Valley, stands as a testament to the timeless beauty of the Rovere estate. With meticulous care and devotion, the "Dama" team cultivates vineyards blessed by rich volcanic soil, the sun's warm embrace and nurtured, by the gentle breezes that sweep through the rolling hills. At Dama del Rovere, there is a harmonious blend of ancient winemaking techniques and modern innovation, with wines that capture the essence of the Alpone Valley terroir. From the delicate whites to sparkling Bruts, each bottle tells a story of their unwavering commitment to quality and deep respect for the land.

Sixty year old vines, hand harvested and carefully selected grapes for the production of traditional method sparkling Durello.

Join us on a journey of sensory delight as you savor the flavors and aromas that dance across your palate, revealing the true character and spirit of Dama del Rovere sparkling wines.

From the vineyard to the cellar, Bubbly Bandit's dedication to excellence is reflected in every sip! We invite you to experience the elegance and grace of Dama del Rovere.
